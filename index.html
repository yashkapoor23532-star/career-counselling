Frequently Asked Questions

Kya Class 6th se 10th ke bacho ke liye foundation course join karna sahi hai ya normal school exams par focus karein?
Dono ka balance zaroori hai. Foundation courses se bache ke analytical skills aur competitive edge (JEE/NEET/NTSE) mazboot hote hain, jabki school exams concepts ko build karte hain. Hum bache ke learning pace ko assess karke sahi sahi roadmap suggest karte hain ki use foundation join karna chahiye ya nahi.
Kya Class 6 se 10 ke bacho ko career counselling ki zaroorat hoti hai?
Haan, bilkul. Is umar mein counselling se bacho ke strong points aur interests ka pata chalta hai, jisse aage chalkar 11th mein sahi stream (Science, Commerce, Arts) chunne mein koi confusion nahi rehta.
Humein career counselling session kab plan karna chahiye?
Jab bhi bacha kisi bade academic badlav se guzar raha ho—jaise Class 8th-10th mein stream mapping ke liye, 12th ke baad sahi college chunne ke liye, ya graduation ke baad career tracks decide karne ke liye. Sahi samay par liya gaya faisla bache ka saal bachata hai.
Kya aap humein sahi coaching ya learning platform select karne mein bhi madad karenge?
Haan, bilkul! Hum market ke alag-alag platforms aur institutes ki detailed analysis (jaise fees, batch size, faculty aur features) aapke budget aur bache ke learning style ke hisab se compare karke dete hain, taaki aapka paisa aur bache ka bhavishya dono sahi jagah invest ho.
